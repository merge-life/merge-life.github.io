Interactive Evolution of Artificial Life Art

Glare E. Dumo

Supervision by Stefano Nichele and Pedro Lind

Welcome to the Interactive Evolution of Artificial Life Art Experiment!

A master's thesis research that focuses on the usage of an online platform for creating art, using tools from artificial intelligence.

The main objective is to provide a way for art creation where any individual - abled or disabled - is capable in expressing their creativity based in simple image selection which is then processed by an algorithm. To Know More

In this survey, you will be provided with two different experiments, each one followed by a short survey. One of these two experiments will use an interactive evolutionary algorithm where the successive set of images will be based on your previous image selections.

Thanks for your participation! :-)
